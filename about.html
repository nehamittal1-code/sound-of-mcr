ABOUT US

Sound of MCR is a platform that celebrates Manchester’s student-led music scene. We're all about spotlighting fresh sounds, new voices, and the local energy that’s shaking up the city.

We host gigs, publish reviews, and feature student artists monthly. From hidden underground sets to high-energy raves — we’re here to cover it all.

Whether you’re an artist, fan, or just curious, Sound of MCR brings the music scene to your feed and into your weekend plans.
